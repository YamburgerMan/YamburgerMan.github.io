Yamburger Recipe


Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes (or yams), peeled and diced

  • 1/2 cup of black beans (canned or cooked)

  • 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs (gluten-free if preferred)

  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh cilantro

  • 2 green onions, finely chopped

  • 1 egg (or flax egg: 1 tbsp ground flaxseed + 2.5 tbsp water)

  • 1 tsp ground cumin

  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika

  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 1-2 tbsp olive or avocado oil for cooking

  • Burger buns and desired toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, avocado, etc.)



Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sweet Potatoes: In a pot of boiling water, cook the diced sweet potatoes until they're soft (10-15 minutes). Drain and cool slightly.

  2. Mash the Ingredients: In a bowl, mash the sweet potatoes and black beans together.

  3. Mix and Season: Add breadcrumbs, cilantro, green onions, egg, spices, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  4. Form Patties: Divide into 4 portions and shape them into patties.

  5. Cook the Yamburgers: In a skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Cook patties for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown.

  6. Assemble and Serve: Toast buns, place a patty on each, add toppings, and serve.
